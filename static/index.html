Pocket To Newsletter

PocketDigest is a personal email digest service that allows you to get the links you saved through the pocket app as a scheduled email digest.

Log in to Pocket

How it works

Login in to your Pocket account

First, you need a Pocket account. If you do not have one, you can start with the sign up process to get one. If you already have a Pocket account, just log in to it and continue with the second step.

Authorize PocketDigest app

We need your permisson to access the links you saved through the Pocket app in order to prepare your email. Therefore in this step we will redirect you to Pocket website so you can authorize our app.

Set your email preferences

After you authorize our app, the Pocket website will redirect you back to our website. You will see a page where you can set all your email preferences such as email address, email frequency, and link count per email. We use those preferences to deliver your links to your inbox.

All done

The links you save to pocket will be delivered in your inbox according to the email preferences that you entered in step 3. Each time we send you an email, we will archive all the links delivered to you.

This is how we make sure that you read a link and you will not receive it again in next email. Note that we never delete links from your Pocket account so you can check your links whenever you want. They are still in your Pocket account. Just check your archive.
